About me

I'm a computer science undergrad, who has a passion for software, web and game development. I started my coding journey in 2016, learning javascript. In 2017 I got captivated by Python, and started C# shortly after that. My specialization in it came around the time I started studying at Utrecht University.

My coding interests are very diverse as I am experienced in many different things. For now, I am not specializing and will continue taking on projects as a developer in many fields. SO if it interests me enough, I will and are able to work on web, game or data science based projects.

I'm open minded, hard working and a team player. I have done freelancing before, but I can also work well with others. Feel free to check my linkedIn below to see my experiences or check for references!

I am licensed in Python Core, Deep Learning and Javascript foundations. I also am fluent in English and Dutch, having an Advanced English degree from Cambridge University. In my free time, I build PCs, draw pixel art, practice the piano and play lots of games. Jedi avenger weeb gym rat ninja, but I always get my stuff done.
